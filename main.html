Flowers are the reproductive structures of flowering plants (angiosperms). They come in various colors, shapes, and sizes, attracting pollinators like bees, butterflies, and birds. Flowers play a crucial role in plant reproduction by producing seeds. Some common types include roses, lilies, sunflowers, and tulips. They are widely used for decoration, gifting, and medicinal purposes. Many flowers also have symbolic meanings, such as love (roses) and purity (lotus).

Roses pictures

Roses are one of the most popular and widely cultivated flowers, known for their beauty, fragrance, and symbolic meanings. They belong to the genus Rosa and come in various colors, each representing different emotions:

  • Red Rose – Love and passion
  • White Rose – Purity and innocence
  • Yellow Rose – Friendship and joy
  • Pink Rose – Admiration and gratitude
  • Orange Rose – Enthusiasm and energy
Roses are used in perfumes, cosmetics, and herbal remedies. They also have cultural significance in literature, art, and celebrations like Valentine's Day and weddings.
